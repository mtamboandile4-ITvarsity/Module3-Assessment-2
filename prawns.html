Prawns are crustaceans with a shell, five pairs of swimming legs (pleopods), five pairs of walking legs, three with claws, and a tail. ... The western school (school) prawn and king prawn are the main species taken. Prawns must periodically shed their shell (a process called moulting) so they can grow.
